Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Darjeeling, Rani's Restaurant brings you an authentic culinary experience with breathtaking views of the Himalayas. Since our establishment, we have been serving traditional delicacies with a modern twist.

Our commitment to using fresh, local ingredients and traditional cooking methods ensures that every dish tells a story of our rich cultural heritage.
